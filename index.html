Introduction

Currently, marine ignorance covers a large part of the population, what is the ocean? What is it for? How do his problems affect me? These are some of the questions that arise when you talk about some kind of problem. The ocean, with its immensity and mystery, is a universe in itself, an inexhaustible source of beauty and life. From the majestic coral reefs to the deep abyss, the oceans are home to a diversity of life forms that defies our imagination. In other words, the ocean is the space of the earth. Our page is dedicated to illuminating the treasures that the ocean houses and highlighting the importance of preserving them since they are an important part of our lives.
